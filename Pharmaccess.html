Pharmacy Accessibility in Iowa

Analyzing access to essential pharmacy services across communities and identifying disparities.

Project Overview

Pharmacies provide access to medications critical for community health and well-being. This project evaluates pharmacy accessibility across Iowa, focusing on vulnerable populations, socioeconomic disparities, population density, and transportation networks.

The analysis identifies areas with limited access to pharmacies and highlights communities where interventions may be necessary to improve healthcare equity.
