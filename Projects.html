Shake Shack’s Network Analysis in NYC

Network analysis of Shake Shack locations in New York City, assessing which stores should close based on service areas, demographics, and median income.

Project Overview

In this project, I performed a network analysis of Shake Shack locations in New York City, evaluating service area coverage, nearby demographics, and income data to identify overlapping markets and underperforming locations.

ArcGIS and QGIS were used to generate walking service areas and visualize spatial overlap, supporting recommendations for location optimization.
