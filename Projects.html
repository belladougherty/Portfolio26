Cedar Rapids Flood Analysis

LiDAR · DEM/DSM · Hydrology tools

Modeled potential flood-prone areas in downtown Cedar Rapids using LiDAR-derived DEM/DSM surfaces, flow accumulation, and hydrologic tools to identify high-risk zones and visualize flood patterns.
