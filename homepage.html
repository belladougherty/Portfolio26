Cedar Rapids Flood Exposure & LiDAR Analysis

Modeling flood-prone areas in downtown Cedar Rapids using LiDAR, hydrologic tools, and land-cover data.

For this project I created high-resolution DEMs from LiDAR data, derived flow accumulation, and identified low-lying areas at greatest risk during extreme rainfall events. By combining elevation, soil, and land-cover data, I mapped flood exposure relative to key infrastructure and residential areas in downtown Cedar Rapids.

Built with QGIS, hydrology tools, and LiDAR data for Cedar Rapids, Iowa.
