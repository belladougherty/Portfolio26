Bigfoot Spatial Analysis

Spatial analysis of reported Bigfoot sightings to identify clustering patterns and relationships with terrain, elevation, and forest cover.

Project Overview

This project examines reported Bigfoot sightings across the United States using spatial analysis techniques to identify geographic clustering and environmental patterns.

Kernel density estimation and terrain analysis were used to evaluate whether sightings correlate with forested regions, elevation, and landscape features. The goal was to explore how spatial tools can be applied to unconventional datasets.
